Privacy Policy

App: Fire Inspection App
Developer: G-Labs Studio
Package: com.glabsstudio.fire_inspection_app
Last updated: March 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

The Fire Inspection App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with third parties. All data entered in the app (extinguisher records, inspection reports, inspector names) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally using an on-device SQLite database. No data is sent to external servers. No account or login is required to use the app.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used for QR code scanning and capturing photo evidence during inspections.
  • Storage: Used to save and share PDF inspection reports.
  • Notifications: Used to send local reminders for upcoming or overdue inspections.

None of these permissions are used to collect personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
